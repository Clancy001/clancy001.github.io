01 — About

Builder of
interactive worlds

I'm Harsh Sharma, an indie game developer and 3D artist passionate about building immersive experiences that blur the line between art and technology.

I work across the full pipeline — game design, 3D modeling, rigging, shaders, and real-time rendering — using Unity and Unreal Engine as my primary creative environments.

My work spans genres: from atmospheric exploration games to fast-paced action titles. I love the challenge of communicating a feeling through gameplay mechanics and visual design.

When I'm not building games, I'm creating standalone 3D art, environment concepts, and character designs that push the limits of what real-time graphics can achieve.
